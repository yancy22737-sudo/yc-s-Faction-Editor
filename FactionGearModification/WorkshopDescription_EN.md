[h1]Faction Gear Customizer - Fully Customize Faction Gear[/h1]

[b]Tired of always encountering pirates with the same crappy weapons? Want the Empire Troopers to be truly fully armed? Or equip a tribal faction entirely with high-tech implants?[/b]

[b]Faction Gear Customizer[/b] makes it all possible! This mod allows you to deeply customize the gear, weapons, and even health conditions of any faction and any pawn kind in real-time within the game.

[h3]‚öîÔ∏è Deep Weapon Customization[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Forced Weapon[/b]: Specify weapons that a pawn kind must carry.
[*][b]Material & Quality[/b]: Set weapon material (e.g., Plasteel, Uranium) and quality (e.g., Excellent, Legendary).
[*][b]Biocoding[/b]: Set the probability of weapons being biocoded to prevent players from easily seizing them.
[*][b]Style & Color[/b]: Customize the visual style of weapons.
[*][b]Smart Conflict Detection[/b]: The mod automatically detects conflicts between ranged weapons and shield belts to ensure unit logic works correctly.
[/list]

[h3]üõ°Ô∏è Apparel & Appearance[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Forced Apparel[/b]: Specify specific clothing (helmets, armor, clothes, etc.) that a pawn kind must wear.
[*][b]Strip Original Gear[/b]: Option to remove the randomly generated clothing of the pawn kind to ensure uniform attire.
[*][b]Color Customization[/b]: Specify specific colors for apparel to create a uniform legion look.
[*][b]Layer Management[/b]: Manage headgear, outer shell, on skin, and accessories separately without conflict.
[/list]

[h3]üè• Health & Implants[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Add Health Conditions[/b]: Add specific health conditions (Hediffs) to generated units.
[*][b]Implant Installation[/b]: Force install bionic legs, tactical eyes, or other implants on certain pawn kinds.
[*][b]Probability Control[/b]: Set the probability of each health condition appearing to create diverse enemies (e.g., 30% chance to carry enhanced implants).
[/list]

[h3]üõ†Ô∏è Powerful In-Game Editor[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Modern Visual Interface[/b]: No need to modify XML files. All operations are done through a modern graphical interface in-game, intuitive and easy to use.

[h2]üìñ How to Use[/h2]

[olist]
[*]Enter the game and open Mod Settings.
[*]Find the editor button for [b]Faction Gear Customizer[/b].
[*]Select the [b]Faction[/b] you want to modify on the left.
[*]Select the specific [b]PawnKind[/b] in the middle list.
[*]Configure the [b]Weapon, Apparel[/b], or [b]Health Condition[/b] for that pawn kind in the right panel.
[*]Click Save, and the configuration will take effect immediately (effective for newly generated units).
[/olist]

[h2]‚ö†Ô∏è FAQ[/h2]

[b]Q: Is it compatible with CE?[/b]
A: I have tested it and it seems fine.

[b]Q: Does it support Mod factions?[/b]
A: Theoretically, yes.

[b]Q: Will this affect existing units?[/b]
A: No. Modifications only affect newly generated units (such as newly spawned raids, reinforcements, or caravans).

[b]Q: What happens if I uninstall the mod?[/b]
A: Only the mod's configuration data will be lost. Game saves are usually safe, but it is recommended to back up your save before uninstalling.
